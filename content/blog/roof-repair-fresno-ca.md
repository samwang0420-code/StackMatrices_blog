## Fresno Roof Repair FAQ

### ### How quickly can you respond to a roof leak?
For emergency leaks, we prioritize your call and aim to provide a temporary containment solution within 24 hours, with a permanent repair scheduled promptly based on the severity.

### ### What are the signs that my Fresno roof needs repair?
Look for: missing, cracked, or curled shingles; piles of granules in gutters (looks like coarse black sand); dark streaks or moss growth; sagging areas; daylight visible through attic boards; and of course, water stains on ceilings or walls.

### ### Can you just repair a section, or do I need a full roof replacement?
In many cases, localized repair is a perfectly viable and cost-effective solution. We will always recommend the most prudent option for your home’s long-term protection. If damage is widespread or the roof is near the end of its lifespan, we will honestly advise you on replacement options.

### ### How does Fresno’s heat specifically damage roofs?
The constant thermal expansion and contraction, combined with intense UV radiation, breaks down the asphalt in shingles, drying out the binding agents and causing them to become brittle, crack, and lose their waterproofing capabilities.

### ### Are your repairs covered by warranty?
Yes. We stand behind our workmanship with a strong warranty on all repairs. The specific duration and coverage will be detailed in your service agreement.

### ### Will my homeowner’s insurance cover the roof repair?
It often does for damage caused by sudden, accidental events like windstorms or hail. We have extensive experience working with insurance companies and can provide detailed documentation and reports to support your claim.
